How It Works

Follow these simple steps to sell your scrap easily and efficiently

01

Sign Up

Create your account on ScrapiX in just a few simple steps. Enter your basic details, verify your email, and you're ready to go. Our secure platform protects your information while making the process seamless.

02

Log In

Access your account securely to manage your scrap sales. View your transaction history, track current pickups, and manage your profile all from one convenient dashboard designed for ease of use.

03

Schedule Pickup

Book a convenient time for our team to collect your scrap. Choose from available time slots, specify the type and approximate quantity of materials, and add any special instructions for our team.

04

Get Paid

Receive instant payment upon collection of your scrap. Our team weighs materials on-site, calculates the value based on current rates, and pays you immediately. Choose from multiple payment methods including cash and digital transfers.

About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Scrap Management

Founded in 2020, ScrapiX is leading the way in revolutionizing scrap collection and recycling. We combine technology with sustainability to make scrap disposal easier and more rewarding for everyone.

Our mission is to create a cleaner environment while providing value to our customers through efficient scrap management solutions. With a team of over 200 dedicated professionals and a fleet of 50+ specialized vehicles, we serve over 10,000 customers annually across 15 major cities.

ScrapiX is proud to have diverted over 50,000 tons of recyclable materials from landfills since our inception, contributing significantly to reducing carbon emissions and conserving natural resources.
